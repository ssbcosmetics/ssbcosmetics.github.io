How Does It Work?

 

You host a swatching party for at least 5 of your friends. You get swatched for free and your first foundation is free! All your friends get a 15% discount on swatching and their first foundations are free. $5 cover charges are always nice for people attending that are not getting swatched. For parties less than 6, everyone will receive a 15% discount.

 

If you would like to host a party that is not in the Triangle, Hampton Roads, or Greater DC/Baltimore area... I need a minimum of 10 people confirmed for swatching.

 

Please contact me if you are interested.

There will be a two week turn-around time before foundations are shipped to the host due to volume.
